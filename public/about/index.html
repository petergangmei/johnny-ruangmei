About Johnny Ruangmei

Johnny Ruangmei is a highly respected futurist with nearly two decades of expertise in disaster management and emerging technologies. Currently serving as the Joint Chief Executive Officer at the Nagaland State Disaster Management Authority, under the auspices of the Home Department of the Government of Nagaland, he has been instrumental in establishing and evolving the institution since its inception in 2011.

Strategic Leadership in Disaster Management

Mr. Ruangmei’s leadership is characterized by a profound commitment to enhancing community resilience and emergency preparedness. His visionary approach integrates advanced technological innovations with robust disaster management strategies, thereby setting exemplary standards in public safety and crisis response. His work not only reinforces governmental protocols but also inspires a culture of continuous improvement and innovation within the sector.

A Devoted Family Man

Amid his multifaceted career, Johnny remains deeply committed to his family. His role as a family man grounds his public endeavors in strong personal values. He believes that the strength of a community begins at home, and his commitment to nurturing family bonds is a testament to the integrity and compassion that drive his leadership style.

Global Engagement and Thought Leadership

A sought-after speaker, Johnny Ruangmei has shared his expertise on numerous international and national platforms. His diplomatic and insightful presentations have graced prestigious forums, including:

  • The American Constitution Center in Philadelphia, USA, during the International Young Leaders Assembly.
  • The Asia Pacific Center for Security Studies at the Department of Defence in Honolulu, Hawaii, USA.
  • The World Meteorological Technology Conference in Geneva, Switzerland.

Through these engagements, he has effectively bridged the gap between technological advancements and policy formulation, fostering global dialogues that prioritize safety, sustainability, and innovation.

Professional Social Work and Advisory Excellence

In addition to his executive responsibilities, Mr. Ruangmei is a committed professional social worker and technologist. He actively mentors startups and advises government bodies on the strategic alignment of emerging technologies with societal needs. His balanced, diplomatic approach to innovation enables organizations to adapt seamlessly to exponential technological changes while upholding ethical and sustainable practices.

Early Career Contributions

Prior to his esteemed role in the Government of Nagaland, Johnny Ruangmei contributed significantly as a State Program Officer with UNDP and as a State Advocacy Advisor with Futures Group International. His early career efforts laid a strong foundation for his current work, where he continues to influence policy and advocate for progressive strategies in disaster management and technology adoption.

Johnny Ruangmei’s career is marked by his unwavering dedication to public service, innovation, and strategic foresight. His professional journey exemplifies a harmonious blend of technological expertise and compassionate leadership, positioning him as a diplomatic leader and trusted advisor in both national and international arenas.
